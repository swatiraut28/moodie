Away from home?


The experience of studying in a foreign country can be challenging and overwhelming, especially for those who are far away from their families, friends, and support networks.

International students may also face a range of difficulties, such as language barriers, cultural differences, financial constraints, and academic pressures, which can contribute to feelings of isolation, anxiety, and despair.

Hello Student!

     

You are not alone!


Depression is a serious mental health issue that can affect people from all walks of life, including international students.

Moving to a new country, adjusting to a different culture, and dealing with academic and financial pressures can all contribute to feelings of isolation, anxiety, and hopelessness.
